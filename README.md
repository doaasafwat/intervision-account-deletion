# InterVision – Account & Profile Deletion Policy

**Last Updated:** August 2026

At **InterVision**, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. You may choose to delete either an individual profile or your entire account at any time.

---

# Delete a Profile

InterVision allows users to create multiple career profiles under a single account.

Deleting a profile will permanently remove:

- The selected profile.
- All interview sessions associated with that profile.
- Interview reports.
- Uploaded CVs.
- Job preferences and profile information.

> **Note:** Your main account and any remaining profiles will stay active.

⚠️ **This action cannot be undone.**

---

# Delete Your Account

Deleting your account will permanently remove:

- Your account.
- All profiles.
- Interview history.
- Interview reports.
- Uploaded CVs.
- Personal information.
- Notification settings.

For security reasons, you must **enter your password** before your account can be permanently deleted.

⚠️ **Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.**

---

# How to Delete a Profile

1. Open **InterVision**.
2. Go to **Profile Settings**.
3. Select the profile you wish to delete.
4. Tap **Delete Profile**.
5. Confirm the deletion.

---

# How to Delete Your Account

1. Open **InterVision**.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Tap **Delete Account**.
4. Enter your password.
5. Confirm the deletion.

---

# Data Retention

After an account or profile is deleted, the associated data cannot be recovered.

Certain information may be retained temporarily if required to:

- Comply with applicable laws.
- Resolve legal disputes.
- Prevent fraud or abuse.
- Protect the security of our services.

Once these obligations have been fulfilled, the remaining data is permanently deleted.
